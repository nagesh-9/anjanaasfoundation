Our Impactful Programs

Through our three main pillars, we strive to create lasting positive change and empower communities.

Education & Empowerment

We believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. Our programs provide academic support and resources to deserving students.

  • Competitive Exam Coaching
  • Career Guidance Workshops
  • Scholarship Assistance
  • Skill Development Training

Career Opportunities

We bridge the gap between education and employment by equipping individuals with the practical skills needed to thrive in the modern workforce.

  • Vocational Training Programs
  • Job Placement Assistance
  • Resume & Interview Preparation
  • Mentorship Programs

Service Activities

Our commitment to social service extends beyond formal education to provide direct support and improve the overall well-being of the community.

  • Health & Wellness Camps
  • Agricultural Support & Training
  • Community Development Projects
  • Disaster Relief & Support

About Anjanaas

Anjanaas Foundation is a registered NGO committed to social service, education, and skill development in Avanigadda, Andhra Pradesh.
